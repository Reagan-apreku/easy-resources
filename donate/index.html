How we stay sustainable

Unlike most platforms, we don't believe in selling user data or hiding professional features behind "Pro" subscriptions. We want the best tools to be accessible to anyone with an internet connection.

To achieve this, we rely on a hybrid sustainability model. Minimal, high-quality advertisements cover our baseline operational costs, while community donations directly fuel the expansion of our toolset and high-resolution infrastructure.

Your support goes directly towards:

  • ✅ High-performance server infrastructure
  • ✅ Research and development of new tools
  • ✅ Keeping the site clean and tracker-free
  • ✅ Maintaining SVG and high-res export capabilities
